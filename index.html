Scan Modes for Every Need

Choose the depth that fits your goal while keeping every scan safe, ethical, and beginner-friendly.

Quick Scan

Fast mode

Best for quick checks and first-time users who want a simple starting point.

  • Fast scan with limited checks
  • Beginner-friendly basic results
  • Ideal for landing page quick scan
  • Safe and non-intrusive review
Recommended

Standard Scan

Balanced mode

The recommended option for most users who want deeper feedback without extra complexity.

  • Balanced scan coverage
  • Clear risk levels and explanations
  • Guided fixes for common issues
  • Recommended for dashboard users
  • Safe and non-destructive analysis

Deep Scan

Extended mode

For users who want broader coverage and more detailed educational feedback.

  • More complete safe coverage
  • More detailed vulnerability context
  • Best for deeper learning and review
  • Supports advanced explanation modes
  • Still ethical and non-intrusive
